My
Story


I have just one story to tell, my journey to an IIT. I was in 7th standard When my mom put me in a coaching institute mainly because I used to study just one hour and still manage to ace school examinations, so my mom wanted me to keep myself busy so, she put me in RESONANCE, to which I agreed with excitement because they teach you extra stuff that isn't in textbooks and I love knowing things. but gosh, it was an amazing experience but from 1 hour-a-day , I went to like 3 hours of self-study keeping school hours aside. My routine became like 8am to 1 pm school, 1pm-2:30pm lunch, 3pm to 8pm coaching,8pm-10pm dinner and enjoyment and 10pm to 12 am homework from coaching and schools.oh yeah it was too much, and that's the most I have ever been in pressure.

But time passed, it made me a better, more knowledgable, and experienced person. Fast forward to 4 years later, in 11th standard, my only goal became to ace JEE. At the start, I wasn't much serious about it, so sometimes I skipped homework which is the "worst" thing you can do, honestly. But my one friend, been a true inspiration. He does homework, so does me, else I would feel this "weird" guilt, and it happens in reverse too; I do my H.W. so does he. Coachings would have been really hard if you don't have a friend . Fortunately, I had friends. You see, you can't be motivated to an exam forever; that's when friends come into play when you hit your low, your friend encourages you, and when they hit their low, you help them. So, for us, it's not like "I cracked JEE!"; it's more like "We cracked JEE!". My friends are now on separate ways, but we still are connected and grateful that we became
FRIENDS FOR LIFE.
